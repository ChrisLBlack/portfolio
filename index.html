Train Scheduler

In this solo project, I used two new technologies to me at the time, moment.js and FireBase. The app takes data from the "admin" or user and adds it to a database. The data is then added back onto the DOM when a child is added to the database. The data appended to the page then includes the time to the next arrival and how many minutes the train is away in real time.
